# 2 Mins

Version: 1.0
Status: Product Definition

---

# Vision

2 Mins is a calm productivity app built around one simple idea:

"If something takes two minutes, do it now."

The app helps users overcome procrastination by offering tiny actions that require almost no mental effort.

It is not a habit tracker.
It is not a to-do list.
It is not a productivity system.

It simply helps people start.

---

# Mission

Reduce mental friction.

Help users complete meaningful actions in less than two minutes.

Create momentum through small wins.

---

# Core Principles

## Simplicity

Every screen must feel effortless.

No unnecessary buttons.

No clutter.

No complicated flows.

---

## Calm

The app should never create pressure.

No guilt.

No shame.

No aggressive notifications.

No "You failed."

The experience should always feel supportive.

---

## Action First

The app exists to make users act.

Not plan.

Not organize.

Not customize.

The primary goal is always:

Start now.

---

## Beautiful by Default

Every interaction should feel premium.

Smooth animations.

Excellent typography.

Thoughtful spacing.

Minimal UI.

---

# Target Audience

People who:

- procrastinate
- have ADHD tendencies
- feel overwhelmed
- struggle starting tasks
- want tiny daily improvements
- enjoy calm and aesthetic apps

Age:

16–40

---

# Problem

People often don't avoid work because tasks are difficult.

They avoid them because starting feels difficult.

The first two minutes are the hardest.

---

# Solution

Instead of asking users to complete big goals,

2 Mins only asks for one tiny action.

Examples:

Drink a glass of water.

Delete ten photos.

Stretch your back.

Clean your desk.

Reply to one message.

Take three deep breaths.

Users finish something immediately.

Momentum follows naturally.

---

# User Experience

Open app.

↓

Receive one simple suggestion.

↓

Complete it.

↓

Feel better.

↓

Close the app.

Average session should be under two minutes.
